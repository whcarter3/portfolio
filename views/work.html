Last Call

Last Call is a search platform focused on Los Angeles bars and restaurants that are open late. Visitors can do simple searches to find business near their zip code. It was built as my 3rd project with a group at General Assembly.

Last Call is built on Ruby on Rails, PostgresQL, along with the Google Maps API. It is deployed on Heroku, and utilizes CircleCI for continuous integration, as well as Rspec, Capybara, and Factory Girl for testing.
